7th-floor coverage — U-Net surrogate

Drag the green transmitters. Path loss comes from the trained U-Net (model/surrogate_unet.onnx) at 3500 MHz; other bands are shifted by 20·log₁₀(f/3500) — exact for the distance term, approximate for wall losses. RSRQ/RSSI assume a fully loaded serving cell (RSRQ ceiling −10.8 dB); SINR treats the other transmitter as co-channel interference, NF = 7 dB.
